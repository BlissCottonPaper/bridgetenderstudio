The Maker

Who Tends the Bridge?

BridgeTender Studio is the independent creative workshop of Marc Simmons.

Across education, games, paper, psychology, and design, his work has taken many forms. This studio gathers those forms under one roof.

Marc is a lifelong educator and coach. Shepherding people across thresholds is his life pattern. This studio is the same work, just in different materials.

Joseph Campbell named the Threshold Guardian — the force that blocks the crossing, the voice that says don't go that way. The BridgeTender is something else entirely. He doesn't block. He enables. He's already on your side.

Some projects became businesses. Some became playable worlds. Some are still on the workbench.

The materials change. The curiosity remains.
